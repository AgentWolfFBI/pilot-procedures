## General Format
- Structure of a CTAF call

## Outbound Calls
- Taxi call
- Entering a runway
- Rolling with intentions

## Inbound Calls
- Between 10 and 25nm depending on aircraft performance
- Position, intentions, ETA
- Clear of all runways

## Circuit Calls
- Joining the circuit
- Base with intentions
